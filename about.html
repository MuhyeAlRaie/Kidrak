About Kidrak

About Kidrak

Kidrak is a digital platform specializing in supporting children with learning difficulties, offering assessment, diagnosis, and educational consultation services based on precise scientific foundations. Kidrak operates as an online platform that allows access to its services remotely, ensuring flexibility and easy access for parents and teachers inside and outside Jordan.

Kidrak was founded and is managed under direct scientific supervision by Dr. Hulwa Hussein, who holds a PhD in Special Education with a specialization in Learning Difficulties, to be a reliable professional reference in the field of assessment and educational intervention.

Dr. Hulwa Hussein

PhD in Special Education – Specialization in Learning Difficulties, with extensive academic and professional experience in the field of educational assessment and diagnosis. She relies on accurate assessment using standardized scales as a basis for building effective educational intervention plans. Among her most prominent scientific achievements is the standardization of the Wide Range Achievement Test, 4th edition (WRAT-4) with its sub-parts (Reading, Writing, Arithmetic), which is used within Kidrak services to provide a highly accurate scientific diagnosis and reliably determine educational needs.

Our Vision

For Kidrak to be a leading platform in supporting children's abilities and learning difficulties, combining advanced digital solutions with field expertise, and providing comprehensive educational services based on scientific understanding and empowerment.

Our Mission

To provide specialized assessment, diagnosis, and educational consultation services for children with learning difficulties, through a digital platform under direct scientific supervision, while providing in-person services inside Jordan through a qualified team, contributing to empowering the child and achieving sustainable academic progress.

Our Values

Professionalism and accuracy in assessment and intervention
Empowerment through focusing on strengths
Partnership with family and educational institutions
Commitment to quality and scientific standards
